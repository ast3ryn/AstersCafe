Gameplay Overview

  • Two doors, one path to the café, one path to nothing.
  • The dessert table brings intense competition and bigger stakes.
  • The Secret Menu offers helpful boosts for the guests.

Choose Your Café

Two doors. Only one leads to Aster's Café. The other leads to nothing. Each door has its own keeper, and one always tells the truth while the other always lies.

Door A

To Aster's Café

Choose this door to earn 1 Star and walk into the café.

  • Participants who choose the correct door earn ⭐ 1 Star.
  • Audience members who gifted their votes to the door keeper or a winner participant earn ⭐ 1 Star with them.
Door B

To Nothing

Choose this door and you leave empty-handed.

  • Participants who choose the wrong door earn nothing.
  • Audience members may vote on more than one participant, but only one winning participant can grant them the bonus star.

How the game works

  • The participants may ask questions to either keeper, then decide whose door they will enter.
  • All audience members can participate by gifting the keeper or one participant to carry their question.
  • Audience members may vote for more than one participant, but only to increase the chance of helping a winner, and they can earn a maximum of 1 Star.

Dessert Table

Reserve a table for two, invite a guest, and step into the three-minute challenge where the guest must choose between keeping their plate or swapping with the VIP.

Reservation Rules

How to reserve Send your guest a Marry Me (5 Buff) to secure the table.
VIP status The person who makes the reservation becomes the VIP of the table.
Match length Each match lasts 3 minutes and is played with two covered dessert plates.

The Dessert Challenge

Hidden plates One plate hides the Sweet Cake and the other hides the Sour Cake, Only the VIP knows which is which.
The guest's choice The VIP tries to convince the guest to take the Sour Cake. The guest must either keep their plate or swap with the VIP.
Results if the guest chooses the Sweet Cake = Guest earns ⭐ 1 Star; Sour Cake = VIP earns ⭐ 1 Star.
